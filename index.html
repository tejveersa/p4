Hi, I'm TJ Singh!

I'm a Computer Science undergraduate from NJIT. My favorite programming language is Python! Right now I work on improving the patient-journey at Express Scripts in Franklin Lakes, New Jersey.

I love teaching, and pedagogy is always on my mind. If you were one of my Python students, hi! Feel free to contact me about anything, always.
